Record of movies I saw

Posted on 2015-11-29   |   In Daily Life   |  

Frankly speaking, I saw a lot of movies when I had my computer. It is a good way to kill time and I love it.

  1. Our Times/我的少女时代 (2015, Taiwan) ★★★★★
    Rating: Douban 8.1/10, IMDB 7.6/10 Date: 2015-12-23

    Love the songs and warn ending. Normal love in school but pure and exquisite heart of a girl.

  2. The Martian/火星救援 (2015, America) ★★★★
    Rating: Douban 8.4/10, IMDB 8.2/10 Date: 2015-12-23

    Fight for survival.

  3. 边境杀手 (2015, America) ★★
    Rating: Douban 7.7/10, IMDB 7.9/10 Date: 2015-12-21

    Not like very much.

  4. The Intern/实习生 (2015, America) ★★★★
    Rating: Douban 7.7/10, IMDB 7.3/10 Date: 2015-12-17

    It is quite interesting to see how a retired person adapt to the new job with a harsh female boss.

  5. 云中行走 (2015, America) ★★★
    Rating: Douban 7.7/10, IMDB 7.6/10 Date: 2015-12

    Breathtaking adventure.

  6. ビリギャル/垫底辣妹 (2015, Japan) ★★★★★
    Rating: Douban 8.2/10, IMDB 5.7/10 Date: 2015-12-06

    A inspiring story about highschool education and fighting for even impossible high target. The leading lady is lovely and worth looking.

  7. Eat Pray Love/美食恋爱祈祷 (2010) ★★★
    Rating: Douban 7.3/10, IMDB 5.7/10 Date: 2015-11-29

    How to balance the life and love. Forgive yourself and find your happiness.

    “This is a good sign, having a broken heart. It means we have tried for something.”

  8. Youth/年轻气盛 (2015) ★★★★
    Rating: Douban 8.2/10, IMDB 7.5/10 Date: 2015-11

    “Youth” make the viewer think about life, old age, ethics, it accompanies us through some beautiful ideas.

  9. Begin Again/歌曲改变人生 (2013) ★★★★★
    Rating: Douban 8.5/10, IMDB 7.5/10 Date: 2014

    So many beautiful songs.
